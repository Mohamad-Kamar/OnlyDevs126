## Visual rules

- Keep the current white, black, grey, and blue visual language.
- Use plain visual first and formal name second.
- Keep the cumulative path subtle; it is navigation, not a second headline.
- Highlight only the stages reached so far. On AI slides, show AI as a layer or force across stages.
- Keep the video card minimal. It should send viewers to the original video, not reproduce it.
- Keep person-in-the-system plants in the talk track unless a slide specifically collects them.
- Use no more than one main diagram per explanation slide.
- Keep source footers small and move qualifications to the speaker guide.
- End on one question. Speak the final line; do not add a second closing slide.
